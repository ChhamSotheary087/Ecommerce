Ordering & Shipping

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. Free standard shipping is automatically applied to all orders over $150. For orders under $150, a flat fee of $15 is charged.
Once your order is processed and dispatched (usually within 1-2 business days), you will receive a confirmation email containing a tracking number and a link to trace your delivery in real-time.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer free returns within 30 days of receiving your order. The returned items must be unworn, unwashed, in their original packaging with tags attached. Please note that sale items or custom tailored pieces are final sale.
To exchange an item for a different size or color, please contact our concierge team at concierge@sovannstore.com. We will initiate a prepaid return label and hold the exchange item for you until the original arrives at our warehouse.

Sizing & Fit

Each product page features a size chart and recommendations based on the item's fit (oversized, tailored, true to size). If you fall between sizes, we recommend sizing up for structured items or checking our detailed fit guides.

Payments & Discounts

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are securely encrypted.
You can enter your discount code at checkout in the 'Promo Code' field. Only one code can be used per order and they cannot be combined with other offers.

Products & Materials

Each item comes with a specific care label. We generally recommend washing at cold temperatures and air drying to preserve the fabric's integrity and reduce environmental impact.

Sustainability & Sourcing

We work directly with certified mills around the globe. Our organic cotton is certified under GOTS, our linen is sourced from Belgian flax growers, and our cashmeres are hand-combed sustainably in Mongolia.
Yes. All SOVANN boxes, garment bags, and tissue wraps are made from 100% recycled fibers, printed with organic soy ink, and are fully compostable or recyclable.
